Social Skills and Making Friends

Presented by The Autism Society of Colorado and Sam Towers


Many individuals with disabilities do not learn social skills readily through the course of natural social interactions. This can hinder their interactions with others and their ability to make friends.

This course is designed for parents, professionals, and other caregivers who wish to increase the positive social interactions of an individual with a disability.

Among the topics that will be covered are:

  • How to determine which social skills to teach.
  • How to prompt social skills effectively.
  • How to provide effective feedback.
  • How to use role-playing.
  • How to use social stories.
  • How to set up opportunities for practicing social skills.
  • How to help the individual make friends.
