Behavior Summit

Sponsored by North Metro Community Services, Inc.

Purpose: To give the participants the skills to do the following when serving individuals with developmental disabilities:

  • To assess behavior and develop interventions for the behavior.
  • To be the "Go To" person on a team regarding behavior.
  • To train others to understand problem behavior.
  • To train others in how to implement behavior programs with fidelity.
  • To serve as a liaison between a team and Board Certified Behavior Analysts, mental health professionals, social workers, and professionals in other related fields.

Trainer: Sam Towers

Dates and Times: This training will be held from 9:00 a.m. until 4:00 p.m. on October 17, October 24, October 31, and November 14. An hour break for lunch will be provided each day; however, lunch will not be provided.

Location: Imagine! at 1665 Coal Creek Drive, Lafayette, CO 80026-2784.

Cost and registration: The cost for the training is $245 per individual. You may register and pay by clicking the PayPal "Add to Cart" button below, or you may send payment to Sam Towers, 36697 County Road 47, Otis, CO 80743.

If mailing in a payment, please include the following:

  • The participant’s name.
  • A phone number at which the participant may be reached if a training has to be rescheduled due to inclement weather.
  • An e-mail address to which a confirmation of registration can be sent.

Note: Although this training is primarily targeted toward those in organizations who serve individuals with disabilities, it would also be an excellent class for parents who want to become more involved and/or knowledgeable about behavior interventions for their child.
