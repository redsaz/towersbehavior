Program Development

Creating or refining programs for specific needs

I provide design/development and consultation services for the behavioral components for new or existing programs. Examples of programs for which I can provide these services include:

  • self-contained programs in schools
  • residential treatment programs
  • daycare or early childhood programs
  • day treatment programs

I have developed and have participated in the development of programs for a variety of populations. They include:

  • school-based programs for students with emotional/behavior disorders
  • residential and day programs for adults with developmental disabilities
  • residential and day programs individuals with mental illness
  • residential and day programs for sex offenders
Using Applied Behavior Analysis to help those who help others.
